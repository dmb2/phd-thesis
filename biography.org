David Bjergaard was born on October 7, 1988, in Dickinson North
Dakota. He earned his B.S. Physics with honors from Johns Hopkins
University in 2011. He completed his Ph.D. in Physics at Duke
University in 2017. He enjoys digital photography, brewing beer, and
writing and maintaining open source software projects.
